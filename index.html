What is SAFDCare



Something the San Antonio Fire Department does not have is an updated user friendly wellness website. These firefighters' mental health, nutrition, and overall wellness is important to not only themselves but their families. Using the SAFDCare website, firefighters and their loved ones now have an easy to use website to better access so many resources! These resources will help give firefighters assessments to better understand their mental health, better sleep, cancer awareness, fitness plans, and so much more!
